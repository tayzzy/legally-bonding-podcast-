About Legally Bonding

Legally Bonding was born out of a simple belief: the law shapes every part of our lives, yet most of us have no idea how it actually works. This podcast exists to change that.

Whether it's a landmark Supreme Court ruling, the fine print in a contract, or a criminal trial that gripped the nation — every episode breaks it down in plain English, with context, nuance, and zero condescension.

New episodes drop weekly — solo deep dives, guest conversations with barristers, solicitors, judges, and legal academics, and everything in between.
